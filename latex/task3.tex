	Ice melting rate can be related to the injection velocity of fresh water $v_0$, by taking into account the expansion of water as it melts. This gives
	\begin{equation*}
	  v_1 = \frac{\rho_l}{\rho_i}(1-s_0/1000)v_0
	\end{equation*}
